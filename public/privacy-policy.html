Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Mojo Yazılım Ltd. Şti. ("Mojo Yazılım", "us", "we", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you access or use our websites, mobile applications, and other products and services (collectively, the "Services"), contact our support staff, or otherwise interact with us.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If changes are made, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, by providing additional notices (such as adding a statement on our website homepage or sending a notification). We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we handle your information and your available choices.

2. Data We Collect

When you use a Mojo Yazılım application ("App"), we or our trusted partners may collect data from your device. This information may directly or indirectly identify you. The data may be stored in identifiable or aggregate formats, ensuring that you cannot reasonably be identified from aggregate data. The types of information we collect include:

Registration Data: If the App offers registration, we collect the data you provide, such as name, email address, and other registration details.

Social Media Logins: Some Apps may allow you to register using social media account details (e.g., Facebook or Twitter). If you choose this option, we collect the information detailed in the "Social Logins" section.

App Interactions: Data is collected when you post, share materials, upload data, request services, or make selections within the App.

Movement Data: With your consent, some Apps may collect movement data from your device.

Geolocation Data: We may collect location data, including precise GPS-level data, even when the App is not in active use. This helps us and third parties provide personalized content or offers.

Social Network Data: Any data you share via linked social media accounts (e.g., Facebook) or public forums, including usage information and connections, may also be collected.

Device Information: Automatically collected data includes device details, carrier information, and technical data.

Important Information About Face Data Collection and Storage We do not collect face data. Photos are combined with our API locally on your device for entertainment purposes. Photos are processed only on your device and are never sent to our servers. TrueDepth API Usage If we use Apple’s TrueDepth API technologies (e.g., ARKit for creating AR effects), the data collected remains on your device and is used solely to provide App features. None of this data is stored, processed, or shared externally. For more information, visit Apple’s TrueDepth API documentation.

3. How We Use the Information We Collect

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and optimize the functionality of Mojo Yazılım Apps.
  • Enable registration and account creation.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Conduct research and analysis about your use of our products, services, or content.
  • Communicate with you via email, postal mail, or mobile notifications about products or services of interest.
  • Develop and display personalized content and advertising tailored to your interests.
  • Verify eligibility for contests and promotions.
  • Enforce our terms and policies.
  • Improve and manage Mojo Yazılım Apps, including combining your data across different Apps for better user experiences.
  • When geolocation data is collected (with your consent), it is used to provide location-based services, alerts, and advertising.

4. Cross-App Advertising and Interest-Based Advertising

We and third parties may use technologies like mobile SDKs to deliver personalized ads and content within Mojo Yazılım Apps. These technologies allow data collection to tailor ads based on your device usage.

Opting Out of Interest-Based Advertising

iOS Devices: Go to Settings → Privacy → Advertising and enable "Limit Ad Tracking."

Android Devices: Open the Google Settings app → Ads → Enable "Opt out of interest-based ads."
